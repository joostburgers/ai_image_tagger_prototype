Bias Types We Track

Ageism

Biases related to age representation, stereotyping, or underrepresentation of certain age groups.

Genderism

Gender stereotypes, binary assumptions, or underrepresentation of gender diversity.

Ableism

Lack of disability representation or stereotypical portrayals of people with disabilities.

Colorism

Biases in skin tone representation, overrepresentation of certain ethnicities, or stereotyping.
