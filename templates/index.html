Bias Types We Track

Age Discrimination

Biases related to age representation, stereotyping, or underrepresentation of certain age groups (Allen et al. 2023).

Race & Ethnicity

Racial biases, stereotyping, or underrepresentation across different ethnic groups (Dancy and Saucier 2022; Yang 2025).

Gender

Gender stereotypes, binary assumptions, or underrepresentation of gender diversity (Gorska and Jemielniak 2023).

Class & Socioeconomic Status

Biases related to socioeconomic class representation and stereotyping (Howard and Borenstein 2018).

We recognize that bias in AI is a global issue affecting communities worldwide (Mbalaka 2023; Vargas-Veleda et al. 2025).
